A place to rest

Pedregoso, Villarrica. La Araucanía, Chile

Lodge Pedregoso

It is a family country house rethought to welcome travelers from the south of the world to rest and enjoy the beauty of its native forests and variety of birds. Pedregoso Lodge is located next to the Pedregoso River, 12 km from the city of Villarrica on the lakes route, which connects 7 lakes in the area.

The two-story house has 3 bedrooms with private bathrooms outside the room, and a bedroom with an integrated bathroom plus a balcony, two living rooms with a fireplace and a workroom, dining room and terrace, central heating, and wood stove. All spaces are very well lit with natural light.

An ideal area to relax, observe nature, and enjoy the benefits of the countryside.
